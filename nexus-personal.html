Common Questions.

Does it work on Mac and PC? +

Yes. It works on both Mac and Windows computers. Newer Macs with Apple chips and Windows PCs with dedicated graphics cards will run faster.

Does it need the internet? +

No. Everything runs on your computer. You only need internet if you want to check for updates or use optional web features.

Can I uninstall it later? +

Yes. It's a regular app—just drag it to the trash (Mac) or uninstall from Settings (Windows). Everything gets removed cleanly.

Will you see my files? +

Never. Your files stay on your computer. We have no way to see them, and your data is never used to train any AI.
