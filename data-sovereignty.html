Data Sovereignty

Last updated: December 2024

1. Your Data, Your Control

At Barrios A2I, we believe in absolute data sovereignty. You maintain full ownership and control over all data processed through our AI systems. We act as stewards, not owners, of your information.

2. Data Residency

All client data is stored in secure, geographically appropriate data centers. We support region-specific data residency requirements and can configure systems to comply with local data protection laws.

3. AI Model Training

Your data is never used to train our AI models without explicit written consent. Client data remains isolated and is never shared across accounts or used for improving general-purpose models.

4. Data Portability

You have the right to export your data at any time in standard, machine-readable formats. Upon request, we provide complete data exports including all processed results and metadata.

5. Data Deletion

Upon termination of services or at your request, all your data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. We provide certified deletion confirmation upon request.

6. Compliance

Our data handling practices comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations. For compliance inquiries, contact data@barriosa2i.com
